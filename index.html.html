Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about CW Suite.

What is the CW Suite Price Connect App?

The Price Connect App is a powerful tool within CW Suite that allows you to monitor competitor pricing in real-time across various marketplaces and e-commerce platforms, helping you make informed pricing decisions.

How does the Price Connect App work?

The app continuously scans competitor websites and marketplaces for your specified products, collecting pricing data and presenting it in an easy-to-understand dashboard with alerts for significant changes.

Which marketplaces can the app connect to?

CW Suite Price Connect integrates with all major marketplaces including Amazon, eBay, Walmart, and over 50 specialized retail platforms globally.

Is the data provided by the app real-time?

Yes, our system updates pricing information every 15 minutes to ensure you have the most current data for decision-making.

How does the app update the competitor data?

Data is refreshed every 15 minutes during business hours and hourly overnight to provide near real-time insights without overwhelming your system.
